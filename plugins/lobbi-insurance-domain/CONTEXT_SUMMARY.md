# lobbi-insurance-domain Context Summary

## Plugin purpose
Insurance agency and MGA automation covering the full policy lifecycle — from new business workflows and claims processing through producer portal design, E&O tracking, renewal automation, and state compliance checking.

## Skill index
- `skills/policy-workflow/SKILL.md` — Design policy submission, underwriting, and issuance workflows
- `skills/claims-processor/SKILL.md` — Build claims intake, triage, and settlement automation
- `skills/producer-portal/SKILL.md` — Design producer/agent self-service portal specifications
- `skills/eo-tracker/SKILL.md` — E&O exposure tracking and mitigation workflow design
- `skills/renewal-automator/SKILL.md` — Automated renewal workflows with retention scoring
- `skills/insurance-compliance/SKILL.md` — State insurance department compliance checklists

## Agent index
- `agents/insurance-architect.md` — Architects end-to-end insurance agency automation solutions
- `agents/claims-specialist.md` — Designs claims processing workflows and exception handling
- `agents/compliance-checker.md` — Validates insurance workflows against state regulations
