# lobbi-compliance-guard Context Summary

## Plugin purpose
Regulatory compliance design for insurance agencies, mortgage companies, and financial services firms operating under FINRA, state insurance regulations, SOX, GDPR/CCPA, and internal compliance frameworks.

## Skill index
- `skills/compliance-check/SKILL.md` — Audit workflows against specific regulatory requirements
- `skills/audit-trail/SKILL.md` — Design audit log schemas for FINRA, SOX, state insurance regulators
- `skills/gdpr-review/SKILL.md` — Review data handling for GDPR and CCPA compliance
- `skills/data-retention/SKILL.md` — Design data retention and deletion policies with regulatory basis
- `skills/sox-validation/SKILL.md` — Validate financial controls against SOX Section 302/404
- `skills/regulatory-map/SKILL.md` — Map business processes to specific regulatory requirements

## Agent index
- `agents/compliance-reviewer.md` — Reviews implementations for regulatory gaps
- `agents/audit-specialist.md` — Designs audit packages and evidence collection
- `agents/regulatory-advisor.md` — Advises on regulatory interpretation and applicability

## When-to-load guidance
Load this summary first. Open specific skill files when performing a focused compliance review for a specific regulation or process.
